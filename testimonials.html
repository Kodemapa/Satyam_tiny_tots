WhatsApp & Telegram Updates

For the convenience of parents, we have active WhatsApp groups and a Telegram channel. We share daily updates, photos of classroom activities, and urgent notices instantly. This ensures you never miss out on your child's milestones.

We believe education involves the whole family. We organize special events like Grandparents' Day, Matru-Pitru Pujan, and Sports Day where parents actively participate in races and fun games.
