Satyam Tiny Tots Bellary

At our Bellary center we provide a rich early years curriculum that combines play-based learning with hands-on labs and sensory-rich activities including Kreedo Labs, senses & feeling games, music & movement, art & craft and guided outdoor play to foster curiosity, social skills and healthy development.

Kreedo Labs
A child-friendly STEM lab where kids explore simple experiments, build things, and learn cause & effect through guided play and discovery.
Hands-on, safe & supervised Age 3+
Senses & Feeling Games
Sensory stations and tactile games help children learn through touch, sound, smell and movement great for sensory integration and emotional vocabulary.
Small groups Creative play
Music & Movement
Rhythm, songs and movement activities build coordination, listening skills and joyful group participation.
Daily sessions Social learning
Art & Craft
Open-ended art, collage and craft projects help build fine motor skills and encourage creative expression.
Materials provided Guided activities
Outdoor & Play
Structured outdoor play and free movement sessions for gross motor development, team games and exploration.
Safe outdoor spaces Supervised
Language & Story Circle
Storytelling, circle time and guided language activities build vocabulary, listening and early literacy skills.
Daily story time Parent updates
