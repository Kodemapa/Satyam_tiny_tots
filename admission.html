Admission to school

Admission to Satyam Tiny Tots is carried out on a first-come-first-serve basis, prioritizing a comfortable interaction process to understand the child's readiness. We believe in an inclusive approach where every child gets an opportunity to shine.

Before formal admission, we hold a friendly interaction session. We won't be testing meticulously; we'll just talk. About what they like to do, what fascinates them, about friends, favorite games, and other entertaining things. This ensures the child feels comfortable and excited about their new journey.

01
Step

Inquiry & Registration

Parents can visit the school office to collect the admission form or register online. A preliminary interaction date will be scheduled. This stage helps us understand the basic details of the child and the family's expectations.

02
Step

Child Interaction & Observation

For Kindergarten, we conduct a play-based observation session. Teachers observe the child's social skills, motor skills, and general readiness. There is no formal written test for toddlers. The goal is to ensure the child can settle happily in our environment.

03
Step

Principal's Interaction

After the observation, parents are invited for a brief interaction with the Principal. This is a transparent discussion to align the school's philosophy with parental goals and to clarify any queries regarding the curriculum or facilities.

04
Step

Conclusion of Contract

Once admission is granted, the final stage involves submitting the required documents and paying the admission fees. After these formalities, you will receive the welcome kit, uniform details, and the schedule for the academic year.

List of documents required

Please submit the following documents to the administration office to finalize the admission:

  • Photocopy of the child's Birth Certificate
  • Photocopy of Child's Aadhaar Card (if available)
  • Immunization / Vaccination Record Copy
  • Original Transfer Certificate (for Grade 1 onwards)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate from a registered practitioner
  • Passport size photos of the Child (4 copies)
  • Passport size photos of Parents (2 copies each)
  • Address Proof (Aadhaar / Passport / Utility Bill)
  • Photocopy of Parents' Aadhaar/PAN Card
Note: The day and time of interaction is agreed with the parents and is usually scheduled in the first half of the day to ensure the child is fresh and active.
