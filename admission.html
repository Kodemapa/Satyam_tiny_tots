How to Verify CBSE Certification

Protecting your child's educational future requires due diligence. Here is your guide.

01
Step

Check the School's Affiliation Number

Every CBSE-affiliated school has a unique affiliation number. This is your first and most important verification step. Look for the complete school name, ensure the affiliation number is active, and verify the address matches the physical location.

02
Step

Verify Affiliation Status and Validity

CBSE affiliations aren't permanent; they come with specific validity periods. Check the "Valid Up To" date on the CBSE portal. Confirm the school has affiliation for all classes they operate (Primary, Secondary, Senior Secondary) and check for any pending conditions.
