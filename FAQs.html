Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about admissions, academics, campus life, and more at Satyam International School.

For Nursery, the child should be 3+ years old as of June 1st of the academic year. For LKG, the age criteria is 4+ years. We strictly adhere to the age guidelines set by the educational authorities.

Essential documents include the child's Birth Certificate, Transfer Certificate (if applicable), previous year's Report Card, passport-size photographs of the child and parents, and address proof. A detailed checklist is provided in the admission kit.

Satyam International School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. We follow the NCERT curriculum, enhanced with innovative teaching methodologies focusing on experiential learning.

We maintain an optimal teacher-student ratio of 1:25 for primary classes and 1:30 for senior classes to ensure individual attention and effective learning environments for every student.

We offer a wide range of activities including basketball, football, skating, cricket, athletics, music, dance, art & craft, robotics, and debate clubs. Activities are integrated into the regular timetable.

Yes, we have a fleet of GPS-enabled buses covering major routes across Ballari city and surrounding outskirts. Every bus has a trained driver and a lady attendant to ensure safety.

The entire campus is under 24/7 CCTV surveillance. We have trained security personnel at all gates, background-verified staff, fire safety compliance, and a dedicated infirmary with a qualified nurse during school hours.
